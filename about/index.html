Zhangxuan Gu
Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science and Technology in Shanghai Jiao Tong University
Zhangxuan Gu is currently a Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science and Technology in Shanghai Jiao Tong University in China. His research advisors are Prof. Liqing Zhang and Prof. Li Niu. His research interests include computer vision, semantic segmentation and machine learning, especially, transfer learning in semantic segmentation.
